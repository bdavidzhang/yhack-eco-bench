Methodology

How we evaluate and score benchmark results.

1

Task Design

Tasks are designed to test specific capabilities with clear success criteria. Each task is reviewed for fairness and reproducibility.

2

Execution

All models are evaluated under identical conditions with controlled environments, fixed prompts, and deterministic settings where possible.

3

Scoring

Results are scored using automated metrics and human evaluation. Scores are normalized to a 0–100 scale for cross-category comparison.

4

Transparency

All prompts, scoring rubrics, and raw results are published openly. We welcome community review and contributions.
